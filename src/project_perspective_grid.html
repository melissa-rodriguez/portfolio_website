GENERATIVE PERSPECTIVE-GRID ALGORITHM

Generative art is a creative process that involves using algorithms, code, or computational systems to produce unique and often unpredictable artworks. Instead of a traditional approach where an artist directly creates an image or design, generative art relies on a set of rules or instructions programmed into a computer. These rules guide the generation of visuals, resulting in a give-and-take relationship between the artist's intent and the inherent randomness or complexity of the algorithm. The outcomes can range from static images to dynamic animations, reflecting a blend of artistic expression and computational exploration.

This project is a curated collection of outputs produced by a "perspective-grid" algorithm I wrote in p5.js. Each unique output will be offered as a limited edition print.
